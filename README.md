**Your Personal Mental Wellness Companion**  
A minimal, distraction-free app to help you reflect, store inspiring quotes, and stay motivated every day.
---         
                 
## üß† Problem Statement       
     
In today‚Äôs fast-paced, high-pressure world, students and young professionals often struggle with mental clarity, motivation, and self-reflection.  
**Reflectify Me** provides a calm digital space for journaling thoughts, saving motivational quotes, and cultivating daily mindfulness ‚Äî without distractions.

---

## üöÄ Features 

### ‚úÖ Authentication
- User sign-up and login system

### üìù Journaling
- Write, edit, and delete daily reflections
- Keep entries private and organized

### üí¨ Quotes System
- Save your own personal quotes (books, movies, experiences)
- Mood Tracker  (happy/sad/neutral)
- Get a random motivational quote from the system
- Surprise dopamine hit to start your day 

### üí¨ Graph System
 - Shows the graph and activity of the user mood based of how many times user have been sad and happy or exicted

> Wake up ‚Üí Log in ‚Üí Get a surprise quote ‚Üí Reflect in a short journal entry + Mood emojis for tracking the mood user is feeling  ‚Üí with a graph showing mood based on emoji and count ‚Üí  Log out feeling lighter
